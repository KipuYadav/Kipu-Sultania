1. Introduction

Welcome to KIPU FITNESS. This guide will help you transform your body with proper workouts and diet plans for both weight gain and weight loss.

2. Benefits of Fitness

  • Improves mental health
  • Builds physical strength
  • Boosts energy levels
  • Enhances confidence and mood

3. Weight Gain Plan (30 Days)

  • Eat calorie surplus meals (Banana shake, oats, peanut butter, paneer)
  • Workout 5x/week – focus on strength training
  • Sleep minimum 8 hours
  • Weekly progress tracking

4. Weight Loss Plan (30 Days)

  • Calorie deficit meals (boiled eggs, fruits, green tea)
  • Daily cardio + light strength training
  • Water intake: 3-4 litres
  • Track fat loss weekly

5. 🏠 Home Workouts

  • Day 1: Push-Ups - 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Day 2: Squats - 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Day 3: Planks - 3 sets x 45 sec
  • Day 4: Jumping Jacks - 3 sets x 50 reps
  • Day 5: Rest

6. 🏋️ Gym Workouts

  • Day 1: Chest + Triceps
  • Day 2: Back + Biceps
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4: Shoulders
  • Day 5: Full body functional + Abs
  • Reps: 3 sets x 10-12 reps for each workout

7. Nutrition & Diet Plans

🔺 Weight Gain Diet

  • Morning: Oats + Banana Shake
  • Lunch: Rice, Dal, Paneer/Chicken
  • Evening Snack: Peanut butter bread
  • Dinner: Roti + Vegetables + Curd

🔻 Weight Loss Diet

  • Morning: Green Tea + 2 Boiled Eggs
  • Lunch: 1 Roti + Sabzi + Salad
  • Evening: Black coffee + Nuts
  • Dinner: Veg Soup or Low Carb Meal
